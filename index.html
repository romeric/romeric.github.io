Hello, I'm Roman!

I am a researcher in the area of scientific computing. I am in interested in developing numerical modelling tools for finite element analysis, high order structured and unstructured mesh generation and high performance computing. You can find more about my research here.

On the mathematical front, my interests mainly lie in

  • Small and large deformation (linear/nonlinear) electro-magneto-mechanical coupled problems
  • Structural vibration, stress and power dissipation analyses for large scale industrial instruments
  • Studying piezoelectricity and flexoelectricity for morphing and energy harvesting devices
  • hp-finite element development
  • Computational geometry

On the software development front, I am mainly involved in developing

  • Data parallel (SIMD) and thread parallel (shared/distributed) algorithms
  • Novel abstraction layers in modern C++
  • Meta-programming techniques and compiler extensions
  • Scientific visualisation and animation algorithms in Python
